About us - White Water Rafting

At White Water Rafting, our purpose is to connect people with the power and beauty of wild rivers through safe, unforgettable adventures. Our mission is to deliver expertly guided rafting experiences that inspire confidence, teamwork, and respect for nature. We believe in our creed of safety first, stewardship always, and adventure for all. Our motto—Ride the Rapids. Respect the River.—guides everything we do, on and off the water.

History

White Water Rafting was founded in 1998 by a group of lifelong river guides who shared a passion for adventure and a deep respect for the outdoors. What began as a small, locally run operation with a single raft has grown into a trusted rafting company known for professionalism, safety, and unforgettable experiences. Over the years, RiverRush has guided thousands of guests down some of the region’s most iconic rivers while remaining committed to preserving the natural environments that make each journey possible.
